How the Grinch Stole Christmas

Comedy, Family, Fantasy , 2000

          

Overview

Inside a snowflake exists the magical land of Whoville. In Whoville, live the Whos, an almost mutated
sort of Munchkin-like people. All the Whos love Christmas, yet just outside of their beloved
Whoville lives the Grinch. The Grinch is a nasty creature that hates Christmas, and plots to steal it
away from the Whos, whom he equally abhors. Yet a small child, Cindy Lou Who, decides to try
befriending the Grinch.
